19. Tuliskan bahan dan cara pembuatan susu strawberry dengan menggunakan bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

Here are the ingredients and steps to make strawberry milk:

Ingredients:

1. 200 grams of fresh strawberries

2. 2 tablespoons of sugar

3. 1 glass of milk

4. Ice cubes as needed

Steps:

1. Wash the strawberries thoroughly and remove the stems. Cut the strawberries into smaller pieces.

2. Put the strawberry pieces into a blender.

3. Add the sugar into the blender.

4. Blend the strawberries and sugar until smooth and well-mixed.

5. Add the milk into the blender and blend again until all the ingredients are well combined.

6. Put ice cubes into a glass.

7. Pour the blended strawberry milk into the glass with ice cubes.

8. Stir or shake the strawberry milk with a spoon until the ice cubes slightly melt and the milk is well mixed.

9. Strawberry milk is ready to be served.

10. Enjoy the fresh and cold strawberry milk as a refreshing drink on a hot day.

Enjoy!

Jawaban:

Here's a recipe for making strawberry milk in English:

**Ingredients:**

- 1 cup of fresh or frozen strawberries

- 2 cups of milk

- 2 tablespoons of sugar (optional, to taste)

- 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)

**Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the strawberries:** If you're using fresh strawberries, wash and remove the tops. If using frozen strawberries, make sure they are thawed.

2. **Blend:** Place the strawberries, milk, sugar (if using), and vanilla extract (if using) in a blender.

3. **Blend until smooth:** Turn on the blender and process the mixture until all the ingredients are well combined, and the texture is smooth.

4. **Strain (optional):** If you want a smoother texture and want to remove strawberry seeds, you can strain the strawberry milk using a fine sieve.

5. **Chill:** Place the strawberry milk in the refrigerator or add ice cubes to make it colder if desired.

6. **Serve:** Pour the strawberry milk into glasses and garnish with strawberry slices or any other toppings you prefer.

Enjoy your refreshing and delicious strawberry milk!

Directions

Prep

Cook

Ready In

40 mIn a wide bowl, crush strawberries in batches until you have 4 cups of mashed berry. In a heavy bottomed saucepan, mix together the strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice. Transfer to hot sterile jars, leaving 1/4 to 1/2 inch headspace, and seal. Process in a water bath. If the jam is going to be eaten right away, don't bother with processing, and just refrigerate.
