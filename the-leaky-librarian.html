3. the librarian arrange the book in the​


Jawaban:

A librarian can arrange the books in the library shelves in a number of ways:

By classification number followed in the library. Most of the large libraries having books on varied subjects follow this method.

By broad categorization by subjects. Medium and small libraries which do not follow any standard classification systems follow this method.

By language, followed by author name, arranged alphabetically. This is seen in libraries having only fictions.

By sections. School libraries can arrange books - for kindergarten, primary, secondary, and higher secondary.

Alphabetically by titles. This is followed in small libraries having books on 1 or 2 subjects.

By accession number. A library in the beginning stage can have this system till the collection becomes sizable to have a classification system

By book size/ color. This is a primitive system

8. Ubahlah kalimat ke kalimat passive 1. The librarian roads the book to the student


Subject : English

Category : Passive Voice

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 8.5

-------------

The passive voice is one of the two grammatical voices.  

In passive voice, the noun or noun phrase that is the object of an active voice becomes the subject.

Construction of passive voice : S + be + past participle  

Function of the passive voice : to emphasize the object and the action instead of the subject of a sentence .

---------------

Answer :

Active : The librarian reads the book to the student.

Passive : The books are read to the student by the librarian.

--------------

27. I told the librarian ... (pembahasannya kenapa jawabannya A)


Indirect Sentence

Kalimat Tidak langsung

I told the librarian ....

Opsi A.

To return the book

Menggunakan { told, said }pada saat berbicara artinya kalimat tersebut menjelaskan bahwa saya berkata kepada pustakawan tersebut untuk mengembalikan buku itu.

Ciri kalimat disini dijelaskan kepada pihak ke tiga yaitu si pembaca (orang ke 3) bahwa sipenulis ( orang ke1) sedang berkata kepada pustakawan ( orang ke dua ) untuk mengembalikan buku itu.

Demikian jelaslah bahwa text tersebut adalah kalimat tidak langsung.
