21. mohon bantuannya bahasa Inggris my sweater is stained. it( need+wash)​


Verbs followed by the TO-infinitive or gerund with a difference in meaning

My sweater is stained. It needs washing.

atau

My sweater is stained. It needs to be washed.

------------------

EXPLANATION

Pada kalimat di atas, subjek it (sweater) adalah yang dicuci, bukan mencuci. Jadi, kalimat di atas bermakna pasif.

Kalimat dengan kata kerja need, require, dan want yang bermakna pasif dapat diikuti dengan to+be+past participle atau gerund.

Contoh:

I need to clean the windows ⇢ active

The windows need to be cleaned (by me) ⇢ pasive

The windows needs cleaning ⇢ passive meaning

---------------

Learn more about need + gerund:

https://brainly.co.id/tugas/8872708

----------------

DETAILS

Subject: English

Category: Structure and Written Expressions

Sub-category: The Passive

Level: SHS XI

Code: 11.5.8 - passive

Keywords: need, gerund, passive
