26. sin 3x = cos ⅓ π ; - π ≤ ×≤ π​


[tex] \text{Himpunan penyelesaian persamaan } \: \sin 3x = \cos \frac{1}{3}\pi \: \:; \: \: -\pi \le x \le \pi \: \:. \\ \\ \text{ adalah } \: \: \left \{ - \frac{11 \pi}{18} \:, \: - \frac{7 \pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{\pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{5\pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{13 \pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{17 \pi}{18} \right \} \: \:. \\ [/tex]

Pembahasan

Trigonometri adalah cabang ilmu matematika yang mempelajari tentang hubungan sudut dan panjang sisi segitiga.

Rumus Trigonometri Sudut Berelasi

[tex] \sin x = \cos \left(\frac{\pi}{2} - x \right) \\ \\ \cos x = \sin \left(\frac{\pi}{2} - x \right) \\ \\ \\ \sin x = \sin \left(x + 2k \pi \right) \: \:, \: \: k \in \mathbb{Z} \\ \\ \cos x = \cos \left(x + 2k \pi \right) \: \:, \: \: k \in \mathbb{Z} \\ \\ [/tex]

Rumus Trigonometri Sudut Rangkap

[tex] \sin 3x = 3 \sin x - 4 \sin^{3} x \\ \\ [/tex]

Diketahui :

[tex] \sin 3x = \cos \frac{1}{3}\pi \: \:; \: \: -\pi \le x \le \pi \: \:. \\ \\ [/tex]

Ditanya :

Himpunan penyelesaian persamaan tersebut.

Jawab :

[tex] \sin 3x = \cos \frac{1}{3}\pi \: \:; \: \: -\pi \le x \le \pi \: \:. \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

[tex] \begin{aligned} \sin 3x & \: = \cos \frac{1}{3}\pi \\ \\ \sin 3x \: & = \frac{1}{2} \\ \\ \sin 3x \: & = \sin \left(\frac{\pi}{6} + 2k \pi \right) \: \:, \: \: k \in \mathbb{Z} \\ \\ 3x \: & = \frac{\pi}{6} + 2k \pi \\ \\ x \: & = \frac{\pi}{18} + \frac{2k \pi}{3} \\ \\ \end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex] \text{Untuk } \: k = -1 \: \: \Rightarrow \: \: x = \frac{\pi}{18} - \frac{2\pi}{3} = - \frac{11 \pi}{18} \\ \\ \text{Untuk } \: k = 0 \: \: \Rightarrow \: \: x = \frac{\pi}{18} \\ \\ \text{Untuk } \: k = 1 \: \: \Rightarrow \: \: x = \frac{\pi}{18} + \frac{2\pi}{3} = \frac{13 \pi}{18} \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

[tex] \begin{aligned} \sin 3x & \: = \cos \frac{1}{3}\pi \\ \\ \sin 3x \: & = \frac{1}{2} \\ \\ \sin 3x \: & = \sin \left(\frac{5\pi}{6} + 2k \pi \right) \: \:, \: \: k \in \mathbb{Z} \\ \\ 3x \: & = \frac{5\pi}{6} + 2k \pi \\ \\ x \: & = \frac{5\pi}{18} + \frac{2k \pi}{3} \\ \\ \end{aligned}[/tex]

[tex] \text{Untuk } \: k = -1 \: \: \Rightarrow \: \: x = \frac{5\pi}{18} - \frac{2\pi}{3} = - \frac{7 \pi}{18} \\ \\ \text{Untuk } \: k = 0 \: \: \Rightarrow \: \: x = \frac{5\pi}{18} \\ \\ \text{Untuk } \: k = 1 \: \: \Rightarrow \: \: x = \frac{5\pi}{18} + \frac{2\pi}{3} = \frac{17 \pi}{18} \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

Kesimpulan :

[tex] \text{Himpunan penyelesaian persamaan } \: \sin 3x = \cos \frac{1}{3}\pi \: \:; \: \: -\pi \le x \le \pi \: \:. \\ \\ \text{ adalah } \: \: \left \{ - \frac{11 \pi}{18} \:, \: - \frac{7 \pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{\pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{5\pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{13 \pi}{18} \:, \: \frac{17 \pi}{18} \right \} \: \:. \\ \\ [/tex]
