6. 1. What do you know about narrative text (definition, general structure, language feature) 2. What do you know about procedure text (definition, general structure, language feature) 3. What do you know descriptive text (definition, general structure, language feature) 4. The important part of writing curriculum vitae


Penjelasan:

1. Narrative text is a text that tells a story. The purpose of this text is to amuse, entertain and to deal with actual or various experience in different ways. It has generic structures such as orientation, complication, resolution and reorientation. Language features include past tense, past continuous tense, action verbs, adverbs of time and place.

2. Procedure text is a text that explains or helps us how to make or use something. Its social function is to describe how something is completely done through a sequence of steps. Communicative purpose of this text is to describe how something is made through a sequence of actions or steps. There are three generic structures in procedure text.

3. Descriptive text is a text that describes something or someone in detail. The purpose of this text is to describe the characteristics of something or someone in detail so that readers can imagine what it looks like. It has generic structures such as identification and description. Language features include adjectives, adverbs, similes and metaphors.

4. The important parts of writing curriculum vitae are personal information (name, address, phone number), education (degree, major), work experience (company name, position), skills (language proficiency, computer skills), and references.

25. watashiwa amerika-jin desu, hajimemashite! what is definition of that sentence? please answer with english language...


The definition of that sentence is Jikoshoukai (introduce). And the meaning of that sentence is "I am American, nice to meet you."

Pembahasan

Arti soal dan jawaban tersebut kedalam bahasa Indonesia adalah :

Soal

'Watashi wa Amerika jin desu, hajimemashlte!' apa definisi dari kalimat tersebut?

Jawaban

Definisi dari kalimat tersebut adalah Jikoshoukai (perkenalan). Dan arti dari kalimat tersebut adalah "Aku orang Amerika, salam kenal."

自己紹介 (Jikoshoukai)

Sederhana saja, artinya tersebut ialah perkenalan atau dalam bahasa Inggrisnya introduce. Fungsi mengenai perkenalan tersebut adalah agar dapat berkenalan dengan orang yang baru pertama kali ditemui, dan agar bisa mengenai satu dengan yang lainnya.

________

Dalam kalimat diatas yaitu 'watashi wa Amerika jin desu, hajimemashlte' merupakan kalimat dalam bahasa Jepang dimana perkenalan tersebut hanya menyatakan mengenai asal warga kenegaraan dan juga mengucapkan salam kenal.

Jika ingin lebih lengkapnya bisa seperti ini :

はじめまして、わたしのなまえは(...)です、(...)とよんでください、アメリカ人です、わたしは(...)さいです、(....)です、しゅみは(...)です、どうぞよろしくおねがいします。(Hajimemashlte, watashi no namae wa (...), (...) to yonde kudasai, Amerika jin desu, watashi wa (....) sai desu, (....) desu, shumi wa (....) desu, yoroshiku onegai shimasu).

Artinya adalah :

Salam kenal, namaku adalah (...), dipanggil (....), (aku) orang Amerika, aku berumur (...), hobiku adalah, (aku) (...jenis status), hobiku adalah (....), senang bertemu denganmu/kalian/mohon kerjasamanya.

*yang bagian titik kosong kalian tinggal isi saja.

27. buat contoh teks ProsedurAnalisalah teks tersebut berdasarkan definition, social function, generic structure dan language feature​


Jawaban:

Example of Descriptive Text:

                                                        My Cat

         My Sphinx cat is the only pet I have. He has a little hair but is not totally hairless as he has a peach fuzz over much of his body. His coat is often a warm chamois. My Sphinx has a normal cat proportion.

       I like his tail although my mom say that it is like a rats tail. I love his usual color varieties including, tortoiseshell, chocolate, black, blue, lilac, chocolate etc. He is really an amazing cat. Believe it or not, he is very intelligent cat. He can respond my voice commands.

        He is really funny as well as my friends get a joke. I love him so much as I love my mother.

1. The social function of descriptive text is to describe everything such as a person, animal, plant or thing/inanimate object with the properties attached to it such as its shape, characteristics, number, size, type, color, etc. so that the readers can know what something (which we describe) looks like though he has never seen it in person.

Terjemahannya: Descriptive Text berfungsi menggambarkan segala sesuatu baik itu manusia, hewan, tumbuhan atau benda mati dengan sifat yang melekat padanya seperti bentuknya, sifat-sifatnya, jumlahnya, ukuran, jenis, warna, dan sebagainya sehingga pembaca dapat mengetahui seperti apa sesuatu itu dari gambaran yang kita sampaikan meskipun ia belum pernah melihatnya secara langsung.

2. Generic Structure of descriptive text:

Generic Structure is a special procedure of writing for a particular text. This arrangement should be written in sequence. The Generic Structure of Descriptive Text:

Identification

Identification functions as an introduction to what we are explaining. This section contains an introduction and an overview of the topic. This section usually consists of one paragraph, located at the beginning of the text.

Description

The description section contains special characteristics or traits contained in objects, people, or animals that we are explaining. This section usually consists of several paragraphs.

Terjemahannya: Generic Structure merupakan tata cara penulisan khusus dari sebuah teks tertentu. Susunan ini sebaiknya ditulis secara urut. Generic Structure dari Descriptive Text adalah:

Identification

Identification berfungsi sebagai pengenalan dari apa yang sedang kita  jelaskan. Bagian ini berisi pendahuluan dan gambaran umum tentang suatu topik. Bagian ini biasanya terdiri dari satu paragraf, terletak di awal teks.

Description

Pada bagian description berisi ciri-ciri khusus atau sifat-sifat yang terdapat pada benda, orang, atau hewan yang sedang kita jelaskan. Bagian ini biasanya terdiri dari beberapa paragraf.

3. Language Feature of descriptive text:

– Describing a specific participant. It has a certain object, is not common and unique (only one). for example: Kuta beach, my school, Borobudur temple, aunt Mary.

– Using adjectives to clarify the noun, for example: a beautiful beach, a beautiful woman, the famous place in Bali island, etc.

– Using simple present tense: the sentence pattern used is simple present because it tells the fact of the object described.

– Action verb: verbs that show an activity (for example: cook, study, run, sleep, walk, cut, eat,  etc)

Terjemahannya: Fitur Bahasa teks deskriptif:

- Menjelaskan hal/benda tertentu. Hal tersebut memiliki obyek tertentu, tidak umum dan unik (hanya satu). misalnya: Pantai Kuta, sekolah saya, Candi Borobudur, Bibi Mary.

- Menggunakan kata sifat untuk memperjelas kata benda, misalnya: pantai yang indah, wanita cantik, tempat terkenal di pulau Bali, dan lain-lain.

- Menggunakan present present tense: pola kalimat yang digunakan adalah simple present karena menceritakan fakta obyek yang dijelaskan.

- Kata kerja aksi: kata kerja yang menunjukkan aktivitas atau melakukan sesuatu kegiatan (misalnya: memasak, belajar, berlari, tidur, berjalan, memotong, makan, dan lain-lain) ·

34. Descriptive textA. DefinitionB. purposeC. Generic StructureD. Language featuresE. Example of Descriptive text​


A. Definition:

Descriptive text is a type of text that describes a particular person, place, thing, or event. Its objective is to create a vivid and detailed picture in the reader's mind through the use of sensory details, figurative language, and explicit descriptions.

B. Purpose:

The purpose of descriptive text is to provide information and create a sensory experience for the reader. It aims to convey a clear and vivid image of the subject being described, evoking emotions, and capturing the reader's attention. Descriptive texts are often used in literature, travel writing, product descriptions, and scientific reports.

C. Generic Structure:

The generic structure of a descriptive text typically consists of an introduction, a body, and a conclusion.

1. Introduction:

- Introduces the subject of the description.

- Provides general information or context about the subject.

2. Body:

- Presents detailed descriptions of the subject.

- Utilizes sensory details, such as sight, sound, smell, taste, and touch, to create a vivid image.

- Organizes the description based on specific attributes or characteristics of the subject, usually from general to specific.

3. Conclusion:

- Summarizes the main points of the description.

- May include the writer's personal opinion or overall impression of the subject.

D. Language Features:

Descriptive texts employ various language features to create a detailed, sensory experience for the reader. Some common features include:

- Use of vivid and specific adjectives to describe the subject.

- Figurative language, such as similes, metaphors, and personification, to enhance the description.

- Sensory details that appeal to the five senses.

- Specific and precise language to convey the details accurately.

- Use of present tense and descriptive verbs to give a sense of immediacy.

- Parallel structures or patterns to create a rhythmic flow.

- Use of adverbs and adverbial phrases to provide additional information and enhance the descriptions.

E. Example of Descriptive Text:

Example: The Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is a magnificent natural wonder located in Arizona, USA. This vast, awe-inspiring chasm stretches approximately 277 miles long and varies in width from 4 to 18 miles. It is a breathtaking sight, characterized by its deep, colorful layers of sedimentary rocks.

Standing on the edge of the canyon, one can't help but feel a sense of wonderment and insignificance. The sheer scale of the Grand Canyon is overwhelming, with its vast expanse and stunning geological formations. The exposed rock layers tell a story that dates back millions of years and bear witness to the forces of nature.

The colors of the canyon walls are a painter's dream, ranging from rich shades of red, orange, and yellow to deep earthy browns. As the sunlight dances upon the surface, the colors seem to come alive, creating a mesmerizing display. The jagged cliffs and rocky outcrops add texture and depth to the panoramic view.

The Grand Canyon is not only a visual spectacle but also a symphony of sounds. The rushing waters of the Colorado River can be heard echoing through the canyon, creating a soothing melody that contrasts with the silence of the vast open space. Birds soar above, their wings slicing through the air, adding to the sense of serenity.

Exploring the Grand Canyon is an unforgettable experience. Hiking along the rim or descending into the canyon itself offers different perspectives and allows one to witness the intricate details up close. The rugged terrain and diverse flora and fauna add to the allure and make it a paradise for adventurers and nature enthusiasts alike.

In conclusion, the Grand Canyon is a natural wonder that captivates the senses and leaves a lasting impression. Its sheer size, stunning visuals, and the sense of connection to nature make it an unparalleled destination for travelers seeking awe-inspiring beauty.
