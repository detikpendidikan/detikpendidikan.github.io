5. Joan has just received an electricity bill of $283.50. This is 32.5% less than her previous bill. How much was Joan's previous bill?


Jawab: $375.6375

Penjelasan :

Current bill : $283.50

Turn 32.5% into fraction : 32.5/100

$283.50  x 32.5/100  = $92.1375

Less than her previous bill, that means her previous bill was more expensive by 32.5%, so you add current bill plus the percentage result :

$283.50 + $92.1375 = $375.6375

18. The method _______ was raised in early childhood can leave a mark, if not a scar, on the person's personality later in life, (A) by which a person (B) of how a person (C) on what a person (D) in which a person


Preposisi di dalam struktur bahasa Inggris tidaklah asing dalam membuat sebuah kalimat. Kalimat yang cocok untuk melengkapi bagian kosong tersebut ialah the method (B) of how a person was raised in early childhood can leave a mark, if not a scar, on the person's personality later in life.

Pembahasan :

Preposisi adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menghubungkan kata benda, kata ganti, atau frasa dengan kata lain dalam sebuah kalimat. Mereka bertindak untuk menghubungkan orang, objek, waktu dan lokasi kalimat. Preposisi biasanya kata-kata pendek, dan biasanya ditempatkan langsung di depan kata benda.

Cara yang bagus untuk memikirkan preposisi adalah sebagai kata-kata yang membantu merekatkan sebuah kalimat. Mereka melakukan ini dengan mengekspresikan posisi dan gerakan, kepemilikan, waktu dan bagaimana suatu tindakan diselesaikan.

Memang, beberapa kata yang paling sering digunakan dalam semua bahasa Inggris, seperti of, to, for, with, on and at,  adalah preposisi. Menjelaskan preposisi bisa tampak rumit, tetapi mereka adalah bagian umum dari bahasa dan kebanyakan dari kita menggunakannya secara alami bahkan tanpa memikirkannya.

37. You and four other friends are going on a camping trip for three days. you went shopping for food and the grocery bill was $385.00. if the bill is split evenly between you and your friends, how much should each person pay?


Rata-rata (mean) adalah hasil bagi antara jumlah nilai (data) dengan banyak data tersebut. According to the question, the conclusion is you and four other friends are going on a camping trip for three days. you went shopping for food and the grocery bill was $385,00. If the bill is split evenly between you and your friends, each person pay $77,00.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui:

You and four other friends are going on a camping trip for three days. you went shopping for food and the grocery bill was $385,00. if the bill is split evenly between you and your friends.

Ditanyakan:

How much should each person pay?

Jawab:

Berdasarkan soal, total yang harus dibayar = $385,00 dan terdapat 5 orang sehingga rata-rata uang yang harus dibayar yaitu:

Rata-rata = [tex]\frac{jumlah\ uang}{banyak\ orang}[/tex]

Rata-rata = [tex]\frac{385}{5}=77.[/tex]

The conclusion, if the bill is split evenly between you and your friends, each person pay $77,00.
