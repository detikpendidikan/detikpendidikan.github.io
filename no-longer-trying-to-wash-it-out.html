6. 1. Why are we suggested to always keep the product in a cool placeA. To keep it safeB. To keep it freshC. To make it coolerD. To make it stay longer​


READING COMPREHENSION

Answer :

D. To make it stay longer​

Explanation

Pertanyaan dalam teks terkait alasan kenapa kita disarankan untuk selalu menyimpan produknya { Air freshener" segar harum} di dalam ruangan sejuk. Ini dapat dicermati pada kalimat kunci yaitu pada peringatan yang dicantumkan dalam label, yaitu :

Contents under pressure  { Mengandung gas }This product contains propellants { Produk ini mengandung propelan. Propelan adalah zat kimia yang mudah menguap }Avoid heat  { Hindari panas }Do not incinerate or puncture container  { Tidak diperbolehkan dibakar langsung atau botolnya ditusuk }

Maka, dari beberapa kalimat kunci yang dijabarkan dalam peringatan, bahwa produk tersebut harus diletakkan di tempat sejuk supaya dapat bertahan lama karena bahan kimia yang terkandung di dalamnya mudah menguap jika diletakkan di tempat yang panas/suhu panas.

Jadi, jawaban yang tepat adalah d. to make it stay longer { untuk membuat nya bertahan lama }

• PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE •

ditinjau dari pilihan jawabannya, ada 2 kemungkinan tense pd kalimat yaitu present continuous tense (S + tobe + v-ing) atau simple present tense (S + V1)

→ jika kalimatnya berupa present continuous tense, maka jawabannya yaitu B. Is meowing, are trying
• menggunakan 'is' karena subjectnya tunggal (hanya 'cat' saja tdk pakai 's')
• menggunakan 'are' karena subjectnya plural (we yang artinya kami / subject jamak)

→ jika kalimatnya berupa simple present, maka yg benar yaitu
« meows, try »
Namun pilihan ini tdk terdapat di pilihan jawaban

Penjelasan:

Creating a presentation on how to wash hands without wasting water is a great way to promote water conservation and hygiene. Here is an outline you can use to structure your presentation:

---

## Presentation Title: **"Water-Smart Handwashing: Conserving a Precious Resource"**

### Slide 1: Introduction

- Title Slide: "Water-Smart Handwashing"

- Subtitle: "Conserving a Precious Resource"

- Your Name and Date

### Slide 2: Importance of Handwashing

- Briefly discuss the importance of hand hygiene for personal health and preventing the spread of diseases.

### Slide 3: Water as a Precious Resource

- Highlight the global issue of water scarcity and why it's crucial to conserve water.

### Slide 4: Traditional Handwashing vs. Water-Smart Handwashing

- Compare the traditional method of handwashing with a water-smart approach.

- Emphasize the benefits of the latter.

### Slide 5: Steps for Water-Smart Handwashing

1. **Step 1: Wet Your Hands**

  - Use a small amount of water to dampen your hands.

2. **Step 2: Apply Soap**

  - Use a minimal amount of soap to create a lather.

3. **Step 3: Scrub Thoroughly**

  - Scrub your hands for at least 20 seconds, ensuring all surfaces are covered.

4. **Step 4: Rinse Quickly**

  - Use a quick, controlled stream of water to rinse off the soap.

5. **Step 5: Dry Efficiently**

  - Use a clean towel or air dryer to dry your hands.

### Slide 6: Tips for Water-Smart Handwashing

- Use a basin or container to catch and reuse water during handwashing.

- Install water-saving devices or taps to control flow.

### Slide 7: Benefits of Water-Smart Handwashing

- Water conservation and reduced environmental impact.

- Personal hygiene without compromising on health.

### Slide 8: Demonstrating Water-Smart Handwashing

- Include images or a short video demonstrating the steps.

### Slide 9: Case Studies or Success Stories

- Share examples of communities or individuals implementing water-smart handwashing practices.

### Slide 10: Challenges and Solutions

- Discuss potential challenges in adopting water-smart handwashing and propose solutions.

### Slide 11: Conclusion

- Recap key points: water conservation, hygiene, and health.

- Encourage the audience to implement water-smart handwashing techniques.

### Slide 12: Q&A

- Open the floor for any questions or comments.

### Slide 13: Additional Resources

- Provide links or references for further reading on water conservation and hand hygiene.

### Slide 14: Thank You

- Express gratitude for the audience's attention and participation.

Remember to use visuals, infographics, and concise text to enhance the presentation. Also, practice delivering the presentation with confidence and enthusiasm. Good luck!
