Pembahasan

Pengertian Decriptive Text

"Descriptive text" merupakan jenis teks atau tulisan yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan atau menggambarkan sesuatu seperti orang, tempat, benda, hewan, atau konsep abstrak.

Pengertian Describing People

"Describing people" adalah bagian dari teks deskriptif yang fokus pada penggambaran atau penjelasan tentang seseorang. Dalam konteks ini, kata atau kalimat yang digunakan dengan tujuan untuk menjelaskan karakteristik fisik, kepribadian, sifat, atau ciri-ciri lain dari individu. Hal Ini mencakup penampilan fisik, seperti warna rambut, tinggi badan, atau mata, serta sifat-sifat pribadi seperti ramah, pintar, pemalu, dan banyak lagi lainnya

1. Penggambaran Seseorang Secara Fisik (Describe Appearance)

Mencakup penjelasan tentang penampilan fisik seseorang, seperti tinggi (Height), berat badan (Weight), bentuk tubuh (Body Shape) , warna kulit (Skin colour), warna mata ( Eyes Colour), warna rambut( Hair Colour), dan ciri-ciri fisik lainnya.

2. Penggambaran Karakter Seseorang (Describe Character)

Mencakup penjelasan tentang karakter, kepribadian, dan sifat seseorang, seperti ramah (Friendly), cerdas (Smart), sabar (Patient), humoris (Humorous), tegas(Firm), atau punya kebiasaan khusus.

3. Penggambaran Sikap Seseorang (Describe Mannerism)

Mencakup bagaimana seseorang berperilaku atau bersikap dalam berbagai situasi, misalnya Ketika menghadapi tantangan, Dia bisa bersikap tenang, tegas, tidak mudah panik dan bijaksana dalam mengambil keputusan.

4. Penggambaran Perasaan Seseorang (Describe Feeling)

Mencakup perasaan dan emosi seperti senang (Happy), sedih (Sad), marah (Angry), takut (Afraid), atau terinspirasi (Inspired).

32. How to made brushing teeth


Brush teeth and gums with the brush head position at 45 degrees in the border areas between the teeth with gums.

Brush with soft movement and play. Brush the outer surface of each upper and lower teeth with bristles 45degrees position opposite to the gum line so that the rest of the food may still slip can be cleaned.

Use the same motion to brush the inside surface of the tooth. Scrub all parts of the tooth surface used for chewing. Use only the tip of toothbrush bristles to clean teeth with light pressure so that the bristles do not bend.

Let bristle brush to clean the crevices of the teeth. Change the position of the toothbrush as often as possible. To clean the inside front teeth, brush your teeth in an upright position and move slowly up and down through the gum line.

Brush your tongue to remove bacteria and for fresher breath. Choose a toothbrush with a soft bristle brush for hard to make the gums hurt and cause abrasion of the tooth, namely the depletion of tooth structure, especially around the gum line.

Abrasion can make the spent acid bacteria and tooth for tooth enamel protective hard coating has been eroded. Replace your toothbrush when the bristles have been damaged and store in a dry place so it can be dried after use.

Never lend your toothbrush with others because the toothbrush contains bacteria that can be passed from one person to another even if the brush has been cleaned.

Use an electric toothbrush for your child so much easier to use. This type of real toothbrush can clean better than a manual toothbrush, but you should consult your doctor about its use your teeth.
