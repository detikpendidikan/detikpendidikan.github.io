3. Dalam bahasa inggris , apa perbedaan kata ''Cactus'' dan ''Cacti'' ?Jelaskan perbedaannya dan sebutkan contohnya masing-masingtolong dijawab dong !​


Jawaban:

Cactus is singular (tunggal), while cacti is plural (jamak).

"cactus" digunakan untuk hanya 1 kaktus, sedangkan "Cacti" digunakan untuk lebih dari 1 kaktus.

Jawaban:

Cactus itu bahasa Inggris dari kaktus yang jumlahnya hanya satu atau tunggal. Contohnya: Andi has a cactus at home (Andi punya sebuah kaktus di rumah)

Kalau cacti itu bentuk jamak dari cactus. Jadi kaktusnya ada lebih dari satu atau jamak. Contohnya: Andi has two cacti at home (Andi punya dua buah kaktus di rumah)

Jadi cactus itu bentuk singular untuk kaktus, sedangkan cacti itu bentuk plural-nya.

Cactus is a type of plant that can store large amounts of water and survive in extremely hot and dry habitats. There are around 2000 different species of cactus that differ in size, shape, color, and type of habitat. Almost all cacti are native to the desert and dry regions of South and North America. Today, cacti can be found throughout the world.

The cactus plant is known for its unique shape and diverse colors, which can range from red, yellow, pink, and others. Many cactus species bloom at night and rely on nocturnal insects for pollination. Cacti are often grown for various purposes, such as property protection from wild animals.

Cacti have varying lifespans, ranging from 25 years to 300 years. One unique characteristic of cacti is their growth and rest phases. During the growth phase, cacti require direct sunlight, high temperatures, humidity, and water. However, during the resting phase, they require less water.

One of the most impressive features of cacti is their ability to adapt to harsh environments and survive with minimal water. This is due to their specialized water storage tissues and modified leaves called spines, which help reduce water loss.

Overall, cacti are fascinating plants with unique adaptations that allow them to thrive in arid conditions. Their diverse species and remarkable survival abilities make them a captivating subject of study and admiration.
