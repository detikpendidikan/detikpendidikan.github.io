16. dialog bahasa inggris lengkap dengan thanks to, because of dan due to


Here is the dialogue with the connectors such as thanks to, because of and due to as mentioned above:

Vitor: What a great season for English football this year, ain't it?

Seamus: Aye it is, thanks to the performance our elite clubs though, English football is making a history. This is the first time that all finalists of Champions League and Europa League in one season come from one country.

Vitor: Really?

Seamus: True, mate. Even Spain never does it.

Vitor: So, why do you think they manage to do so?

Seamus: I suppose those English finalists, Liverpool, Tottenham, Arsenal and Chelsea gain their success because of their managers.

Vitor: Hmm, I couldn't agree more, but is it really the only thing which triggers such achievement?

Seamus: I believe so. In UCL, Klopp and Pocchettino are undeniably better managers than Valverde of Barcelona and Erik Ten Hag of Ajax. Due to their excellent tactics, Liverpool and Tottenham could really turn over their disadvantage in the first leg into a great victory.

Vitor: I see your point. Not to mention that the EPL always provide a regularly competitive matches in almost every week, literally. They get used to it. What about in Europa League?

Seamus: The way I see it, it's the experience that makes the difference. Unai Emery holds the record as the most manager ever win Europa League in history with Sevilla before. So, it's Arsenal's advantage. Meanwhile, Maurizio Sarri is more experienced in dealing with the rivals in this competition for non elite teams. He worked for Napoli before and did fine. Whereas, Chelsea has better squad than his previous team. So, ideally he should do better as well.

Pembahasan:

Those connectors mentioned above are useful to make complex sentences with their own function.

'Thanks to' is normally stated to express your gratitude to other parties. The pattern is 'Thanks to + noun, clause' .The example is

        'Thanks to your contribution, I was able to get this far.'

        'Thanks to the new regulation, smoking is finally banned.'

'Because of' and 'Due to' is commonly used to state reason on something. The pattern is 'S + V + O + complement because of + noun'   and 'Due to + noun, clause'. The example are as follows                            

        'They are late because of getting stuck in a long traffic jam.'

        'Due to her persistence, he finally gave in.'

PEMBAHASAN:

GRATITUDE (THANKS)

Gratitude is an expression that we show or say to express grateful feeling to other people, when speaking English, you say “thanks” very often. Please say “thank you” when people give you something and give you compliment, etc.

(Expressing Gratitude adalah ekspresi yang kita tunjukkan atau katakan untuk mengungkapkan perasaan berterima kasih kepada orang lain. Ketika berbicara bahasa Inggris, Anda mengatakan “terima kasih” berkali. Katakanlah “terima kasih” ketika orang memberikan sesuatu dan memberikan pujian, dll)

Ekspresi trimakasih (Thanks) adalah sesuatu yang sangat penting , karena ini menunjukkan sebuah penghargaan atau apresiasi kepada orang lain yang telah melakukan sesuatu untuk kita atau membantu kita.

Dan yang terlebih penting adalah ungakapan terimakasih itu diungkapkan dengan mimic atau air muka atau ekspressi yang indah dan menyejukkan, yang pasti dengan senyuman. Karena ini menunjukkan sebuah ucapan atau ungkapan yang tulus dari kita kepada orang laian.

Berikut ini adalah beberapa ungkapan gratitude (Thanks) yang biasa digunakan dalam bahasa inggris.

I’m so grateful for…. (Saya sangat berterimakasih atas/untuk...) Thank you very much for… (Terima kasih banyak atas...)  Thank you for… ( Terima kasih atas... ) That was nice of you. Thank you. (Kamu baik sekali. Terima kasih) Thanks a lot for…. (Terima kasih atas...) I really appreciate… (saya sungguh menghargai) Thanks./many thanks. (terima kasih/ terima kasih banyak) Thanks a million. (berjuta-juta terima kasih  Thanks a lot. (terima kasih banyak) Many thanks (terima kasih banyak) It means a lot to me (Itu sangat bermakna bagi saya) I can’t repay you for what you have done (saya tidak dapat membayar mu atas apa yang telah kamu lakukan) I just can’t thank you enough (Saya tidak cukup hanya berterima kasih) You have been very helpful (kamu sudah sangat membantu)

Expressing Gratitude

Expressing gratitude adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan ucapan terimakasih kepada seseorang yang telah berjasa/ membantu kita. Berikut ini adalah ungkapan-ungkapan yang digunakan dalam expressing gratitude:

Thank you very much Thanks a lot I should like to express my gratitude for.. I should like to express my appreciation for … I’m really obligated to you for … I’m really grateful to you for …

Untuk merespon ucapan atau ungkapan expressing gratitude adalah:

You are welcome Don’t mention it It’s my pleasure No problem Not at all That’s alright.
Pembahasan

Contoh dialog Bahasa Inggris yang lengkap dengan Thanks to (expressing gratitude) adalah:

A: Good morning. May I come in?

B: Please come in.

A: I am Katy, your next door neighbor. I am an officer in the library.

B: Very glad to meet you. I am Perry. Please sit down.

A: Last night my husband told me that new tenants have moved in here. So I thought I would look you up in the morning.

B: So kind of you. I am not a tenant, though. I bought this house.

A: Really? That is wonderful. I am so glad we are getting good neighbors. Is there anything I can do for you?

B: Yes. Could you tell me where I can buy fresh milk in the morning?

A: Please do not worry about that. I will send my milkmen here. Is there anything else?

B: Not immediately. Thank you so much for the help.

A: It is my pleasure. Please do not hesitate to tell me if you need anything.

B: Sure. You are very nice.

A: Not at all. See you later.

B: See you.
