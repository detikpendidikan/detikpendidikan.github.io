30. We wash the clothes together every Sunday. ( - )​


Negative sentence :

We don't wash clothes together every Sunday.

NOTE :

Negative sentence is sentence which use "not", such as :

Do notwas notwere notare notis notam nothave not

We use don't in this sentences because 'wash' is an example of verb.

If we want to change negative sentences with the same meaning, we can do this :

For example :

I don't have any friends. (this is negative)

We can change to this :

I have no friends.

35. b.(+) she plays the piano(+)....c.(+)we drink coffe together(+)....​


Jawaban:

b. (+) She plays the piano

(-) She does'nt play the piano

(?) Does she play the piano?

c. (+) We drink coffee together

(-) We don't drink coffee together

(?) Do we drink coffee together?

Penjelasan:

Simple present tense

(+) S + V1 + O

(-) S + don't/doesn't + V1 + O

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O ?

*Do untuk Subjek (I, you, they, we)

Does untuk Subjek (he, she, it)

*khusus kalimat positif simple present tense dengan subjek (he, she, it) ditambah s/es dibelakang kata kerjanya.

Ini adalah kalimat aktif simple present tense.

Bentuk negatif kalimat aktif the simple present tense =

SUBJECT DO / DOES NOT VERB 1.

DO untuk. I / You / We / They

DOES untuk He / She / It.

1. She does not play the piano.

2. We do not drink coffee together.
