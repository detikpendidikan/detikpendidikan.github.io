26. question for descriptive text eiffel tower



The Eiffel Tower (French: La tour Eiffel. Nickname La Dame de fer, the iron lady) is an 1889 iron lattice tower located on the Champ de mars in Paris that has become both a global iron of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world. The tallest building in Paris, it is the most visited paid monument in the world million of people ascend it every year . Named the designer, engineer Gustave eiffel, the tower was built as the entrance arch to the 1889 world fair.
The tower has three levels for visitor. Tickets can be purchased to ascend by stairs or lift, to the first and second levels . The walk to the first levels is over 300 steps, as is the walk from the first to the second level. The third and the highest level is accessible only by lift. Both the first and the second levels feature restaurants.
The tower has become the most prominent symbol of both Paris and French, often in the establishing shot of films set in the city. That is the most value able experience in my life go to the most – visited paid monument in the world.

32. hape you everbeen to eiffel tower? it is ... then pisa tower( beautifull)​


Have you ever been to Eiffel Tower? It is (more beautiful) than Pisa Tower

Ini adalah bentuk kalimat yang berisi kata sifat comparative, dilihat dari adanya kata than pada kalimat. Jadi, jawabannya ialah:

Have you ever been to The Eiffel Tower? It is more beautiful than The Tower of Pisa.

-----------------------------------------------------------

Lebih baik menambahkan artikel "the" untuk sesuatu (kata benda) yang hanya berjumlah satu di seluruh dunia.

Nama resmi Menara Pisa dalam Bahasa Inggris adalah: The Leaning Tower of Pisa atau lebih singkatnya, The Tower of Pisa.

Bentuk comparative dari kata sifat beautiful adalah "more beautiful".
