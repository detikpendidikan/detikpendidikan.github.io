8. Employee: OK, boss.The modal verb to fill in the blank is ....mustmusn'tshouldshouldn'td.Sari : My back is hurt.Ayu : You s ee a doctorabout it when you havetime.The modal verb to fill in the blank is​


Modals are helping verbs which are used with the principal verb to express certain moods like ability, obligation, request, suggestion, and permission.

Sari : My back is hurt.

Ayu : You .... see a doctor  about it when you have  time.

The modal verb to fill in the blank is C. Should

Pembahasan

Sari : My back is hurt.

Punggung saya sakit

Ayu : You .... see a doctor  about it when you have  time.

Kamu ... mengunjungi dokter ketika kamu ada waktu

The modal verb to fill in the blank is C. Should

Karena memberikan saran, maka modal yang tepat adalah should

Types of modal:

1. Can -> to express the ability to do something in the present

She can play a guitar (she has the ability to play a guitar)

2. Could -> to express ability in weaker manner

i could read without eyeglasses (she has weaker ability of reading without eyeglasses)

3. May -> to express permission, wish or possibility

It may rain today (there is a possibility to rain today)

4. Might -> It shows possibility but it is less than may

I might get sick (there is less possibility of getting sick)

5. Must -> it shows force or necessity, advice or recommendation

I must go now (it shows necessity to go now)

6. Will -> it express prediction or request

I will be rich someday (It shows prediction that I will be rich in the future)

7. Should -> It shows probability, necessity, and suggestion

She should attend the party earlier (it shows that she necessity to attend the party earlier)

34. dialog bahasa Inggris ti get attention, to check if we are understood, to show appreciation to others, and to tell what ee or other people think of something​


Jawaban:

**Teacher:** Good morning, everyone! I'm excited to announce that our school is hosting a talent show next month. This is a great opportunity for all of you to showcase your talents.

**Student 1:** Wow, that's awesome! I think we should create colorful posters to get attention from students and teachers. We can also spread the word on social media.

**Teacher:** Excellent idea, Student 1! Your enthusiasm is contagious. Now, let's do a quick check to ensure everyone understands the guidelines for participating in the talent show.

**Student 2:** Sure! Participants should prepare a performance that's no longer than 5 minutes. They can sing, dance, do magic tricks, or anything that showcases their talents, right?

**Teacher:** That's right, Student 2! You've got it perfectly. Now, let's take a moment to show appreciation to our student council members who have been working hard to organize this event.

**Student 3:** I want to express my gratitude to the student council for their dedication. Organizing a talent show must be challenging, and we appreciate your efforts!

**Teacher:** Thank you, Student 3, for acknowledging their hard work. Your support means a lot. Lastly, let's discuss the theme for this year's talent show. What do you all think about the theme "Celebrating Diversity"?

**Student 4:** I think it's a fantastic idea. It allows us to showcase a wide range of talents and cultures, highlighting the uniqueness of our school community.

**Teacher:** Well put, Student 4. It's clear that the theme resonates with everyone. Your input is valuable in shaping the event. Together, we can create a memorable talent show that celebrates our school's diversity.

Effective communication, collaboration, and showing appreciation are key aspects of making school events successful and fostering a positive school environment.
