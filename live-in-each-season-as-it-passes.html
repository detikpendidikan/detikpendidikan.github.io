38. it rains much almost every day in rainy season (-)(+)​


Jawaban:

Mengubah simple present tense positif ke dalam simple present tense negatif dan interogatif :

(+) it rains much almost every day in rainy season.

(-) it doesn't rain much almost every day in rainy season.

(?) Does it rain much almost every day in rainy season?

Penjelasan :

Simple present tense

♣Rumus/Formula :♣

Kalimat verbal

Positif : S + verb-1 + ONegatif : S + don't/doesn't + verb-1 + OInterogatif : Do/Does + S + verb-1 + O + ?

Kalimat nonverbal

Positif : S + is/am/are + non-verbNegatif : S + is/am/are + not + non-verbInterogatif : Is/am/are + S + non-verb +?

♠Contoh :♠

(+) She is a teacher.(?) Is she a teacher?(-) She isn't a teacher.(+) I go to your house.(?) Do I go to your house?(-) I don't go to your house.

♣Time signal/keterangan waktu :♣

Every day/month/....At seven/nine/....In tne morning/afternoon/....Dll
